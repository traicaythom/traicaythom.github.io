About Us

Trai Cay Thom Co., Ltd focuses on farming and exporting Vietnamese dragon fruits to European markets, such as Netherlands, Germany, and Switzerland. Our main office located in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam. We are one of Vietnam's leading agricultural companies specializing in growing, processing, and exporting authentic Vietnamese dragon fruits, or pitahaya. Our local farms located about 200 km northeast of Ho Chi Minh city, in Binh Thuan province. This province is well-known for the hot and dry weather which is the best condition to grow dragon fruit and its family of succulents. Additionally, its semi-dessert geology is also the key factor to produce the most tasteful dragon fruits in Vietnam. Our Pitahaya is high-quality and cultivated in the farms certified by GLOBALG.A.P. Combining these factors and the location near Vietnam's biggest metropolitan, our farms can provide and deliver the best dragon fruits conveniently to the worldwide markets. With an excellent track record for the best customer satisfaction for the last 20 years, we have never compromised on the quality of our products provided to the customer.

Our main goal is to bring the best pitahayas to people around the world. The pitahayas we provided are certified by the European Commission as fresh and non-GMO products. We also take special request for producing naturally-grown organic pitahayas. For the past decade, we have been partnered with many fruit and vegetable businesses in Asia and Europe countries. Our main customers are Europian markets, including the Netherlands, Germany, and Switzerland. In the future, we plan to widen our market to Japan, and North America. Please contact us to get more information on our best and fresh pitahayas.

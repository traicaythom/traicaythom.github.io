White Dragon Fruit

White Pitaya

  • The white dragon fruit, or white pitaya, is the most popular type of dragon fruits in Vietnam. It is often cultivated in the central part of Vietnam, since it grows well in the sandy soil with the hot and dry weather. The fruit has plenty of beneficial vitamins and nutritional components. Each 100g of white dragon fruit has 40 kcal, where the water component accounted for 87.6%.

  • Additionally, the fiber ingredient in the white dragon fruit is relatively high compared to other fruits. It includes two types of beneficial fibers: insoluble fiber (cellulose) and soluble fiber (pectin). These fibers regulate the activity of the digestive system, reducing hazardous substances to the body, such as fat, cholesterol, toxins, and reducing the risk of acne, boils. The white dragon fruit contains no fat and could be added to any healthy diet.

Farm and Nursery

Binh Thuan, Vietnam

Agricultural Facility for Growing Dragon Fruit Plants

  • Our farm and nursery located about 200 km northeast of Ho Chi Minh city, in Binh Thuan province, Vietnam. This province is well-known for the hot and dry weather which is often considered to be the optimal condition to grow the white dragon fruit plants. Additionally, its semi-dessert geology is also the key factor to produce the most tasteful dragon fruits in Vietnam. Combining these factors and the location near Vietnam's biggest metropolitan, our farms can provide and deliver fresh dragon fruits conveniently to the worldwide markets.


  • Dragon fruit naturally bears fruits for 5 months every year, usually from early summer through mid-fall. It begins flowering in early summer, typically in June, with fruit formation occurring shortly afterward. With new lighting technology and biological understanding of dragon fruit, we succeed in producing dragon fruit all year long. This achievement also thanks to the suitable geology and weather condition in Binh Thuan province.

  • In more than 20 years of growing dragon fruit plants, we always consider quality over quantity. At the early stage of the fruits, our experienced farmers will pick out only the best quality sentiments to let them grow to full size. This method helps prevent the tree from weakening while providing it with sufficient energy to produce the fruit. As a result, only high-quality and hand-picked fruits are collected and delivered to customers.

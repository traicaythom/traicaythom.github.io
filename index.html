About Us

With more than 18 years of experience, Trai Cay Thom Co., Ltd focuses on farming and exporting Vietnamese pitahaya to European markets, such as Netherlands, Germany, and Switzerland. Our Pitahaya is high-quality and cultivated in the farms certified by GLOBALG.A.P. The main goal is to deliver the best experience to the customers through our authentic Vietnamese dragon fruits.
